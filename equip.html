Cooking Equipments

The list contains Non-commercial as well as commercial equipments used in cooking.

Equipment Use Cooking method
Oven Commercial and home Baking and heating
Grill Mostly commercial Griiling and smoking
Deep Fryer Commercial Deep Frying
Salamander or Broiler Commercial Cooking, Finishing dishes
Toaster Commercial and Non-commercial Toasting
Microwave Commercial and home Heating
Mixers Commercial and home Mixing and grinding
Chef Knives Commercial and home Cutting
Blenders Mostly commercial Blending
Food Pans Mostly commercial Used in oven or food warmer
